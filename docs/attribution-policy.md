# Attribution policy

Do not assume every line in an interview, podcast, or panel belongs to the founder.
Segments identify founder, interviewer, host, moderator, panelist, audience member,
narrator, or unknown. Low-confidence speaker assignments require human review and
remain answer-ineligible. Questions and contextual remarks may be retained, but are
clearly secondary.

Answers use third-person, evidence-qualified wording and dates. Direct quotations
must match source text and use short excerpts. The platform never speaks as a
founder, infers private beliefs, or guarantees that an old statement is current.
