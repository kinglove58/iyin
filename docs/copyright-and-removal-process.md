# Copyright and removal process

The public product displays metadata, links, and short supporting excerpts rather
than reproducing full works. Raw artifacts are access-controlled evidence records,
not a public republishing library.

Anyone may submit a correction with category `copyright_concern` or
`removal_request`. An administrator records identity/authority evidence when
appropriate, disables answer eligibility while urgent claims are reviewed, checks
mirrors and derived chunks, records the decision in audit logs, and responds through
the supplied contact channel. Preservation duties and lawful removal requirements
must be reconciled by an authorized operator; deletion is never performed silently.
