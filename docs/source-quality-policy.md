# Source quality policy

Tier A is direct founder speech or writing. Tier B is a reliable interview, event,
or publication. Tier C supports discovery only. Tier D is rejected or unreliable.
Normal belief-oriented answers require approved Tier A/B evidence and verified
speaker/author eligibility.

Candidate scores rank review work using directness, authority, length, transcript
availability, recency, relevance, uniqueness, original availability, duplicate
risk, extraction feasibility, and transparency. Every component is shown to the
reviewer. Scores never approve content. Quality review also checks dates, provenance,
rights, meaningful extraction, conflicts, and independence of underlying works.
